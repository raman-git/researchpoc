Canadian Oil and Gas: How Much Can Canada Cut?

April 7, 2020 09:00 PM GMT
Benny Wong, Stephen Byrd

We see risk of initial supply cuts of ~900 Mbbl/d, potentially increasing to ~1.5 MMbbl/d+ with low oil prices and intensifying storage congestion. Cost structures will determine where cuts will come from yet also the ability to place barrels, giving Integrateds a relative advantage.
